Product Description

  • Shreem Enzymax Plus is a scientifically developed enzyme-based feed supplement designed to enhance digestion and improve nutrient absorption in cattle. It contains a balanced blend of essential digestive enzymes that help break down complex feed components, optimize rumen function, and increase feed efficiency. Regular use supports better milk yield, improved growth, and overall animal health by reducing digestive disorders and maximizing nutrient availability. Ideal for all types of dairy and farm animals, Shreem Enzymax Plus ensures better performance, productivity, and profitability for farmers.

  • Boosts energy utilization and animal performance.

Advantages
