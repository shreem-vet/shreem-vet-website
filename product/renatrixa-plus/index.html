Product Description

  • Shreem Renatrixa Plus is a scientifically developed kidney acidifier and anti-gout formulation designed to maintain optimal renal function and support uric acid balance in poultry. It plays a crucial role in preventing and managing gout conditions, improving kidney health, and enhancing overall bird performance. In modern poultry farming, high-protein diets, rapid growth rates, and environmental stress can lead to kidney overload and uric acid accumulation, resulting in gout and reduced productivity. Shreem Renatrixa Plus helps in maintaining proper acid-base balance, promotes efficient excretion of uric acid, and supports healthy kidney function. Its advanced formulation works to detoxify the system, reduce metabolic stress, and improve hydration status, ensuring better feed efficiency, growth, and livability. Regular use helps birds stay active, reduces mortality associated with kidney disorders, and supports consistent and profitable poultry production.
