Product Description

  • Shreem Coccikill is an advanced and highly effective poultry health supplement specially formulated for the prevention and control of coccidiosis in poultry. Developed by Shreem Vet Industries, it offers a reliable and sustainable solution to manage intestinal health and improve overall bird performance. Coccidiosis is one of the most common and economically significant diseases affecting poultry, leading to poor growth, reduced feed efficiency, and increased mortality. Shreem Coccikill works by targeting and controlling coccidial infection, thereby supporting better nutrient absorption and faster growth.

Key Benefits

  • Effective prevention and control of coccidiosis.
  • Helps reduce intestinal lesions and oocyst shedding.
  • Protects against secondary bacterial infections.
  • Enhance gut integrity and nutrient absorption.
  • Boosts immune response with vitamins and probiotics.

Dosage

  • Prevention: 10ml/100 birds daily (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Treatment: 30ml/100 birds daily for 3-5 days (or as directed by veterinarian)
