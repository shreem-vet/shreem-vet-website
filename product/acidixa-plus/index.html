Product Description

  • Shreem Acidixa Plus is a scientifically advanced water acidifier specially formulated to improve gut health, enhance digestion, and control harmful microbial load in poultry. It contains a balanced blend of organic and inorganic acids that effectively reduce water pH, creating an unfavorable environment for pathogenic bacteria while promoting beneficial gut microflora. This powerful formulation supports better nutrient absorption, improves feed efficiency, and helps birds perform optimally under stress conditions such as heat, vaccination, and disease challenges.

Key Benefits

  • Maintains healthy intestinal pH.
  • Supports optimum performance & enzyme activity.
  • Controls harmful bacteria like salmonella & E.coli.
  • Improves digestion & nutrient absorption.
  • Promotes eggshell quality & overall growth.

Dosage

  • For Feed (Broilers/Layers): 50ml-1ltr per ton of feed (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Breeders: 1-1.5ltr per ton of feed (mix the volume with 5-15ltr of water & sprinkle on feed for uniform mixing)
  • For Drinking Water: 0.25-0.5ml per ltr of drinking water (or as directed by veterinarian)
