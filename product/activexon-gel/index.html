Product Description

  • Shreem Activexon Gel is a highly effective, fast-acting nutritional gel formulated to provide instant energy and metabolic support in cattle. Designed with a powerful blend of essential nutrients, vitamins, and energy boosters, it helps animals quickly overcome stress, weakness, and energy deficiency conditions. This advanced formulation is ideal for lactating dairy animals, freshly calved cows, and weak or off-feed cattle, supporting rapid recovery, improved stamina, and enhanced productivity. It plays a crucial role during periods of stress such as calving, transportation, illness, or sudden drop in feed intake. With its easy-to-administer gel form and high palatability, Shreem Activexon Gel ensures quick absorption and immediate action, helping maintain optimal performance, better milk yield, and overall animal health.
