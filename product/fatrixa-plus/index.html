Product Description

  • Shreem Fatrixa Plus is a premium quality bypass fat supplement specially designed to meet the high energy demands of dairy cattle during early lactation and peak production stages. Formulated with rumen-protected fat, it ensures efficient energy delivery without disturbing normal rumen function. This advanced formula helps improve milk yield and milk fat percentage while preventing negative energy balance, body weight loss, and metabolic disorders like ketosis. It also supports better reproductive performance and overall health in high-yielding animals. With consistent supplementation, Shreem Fatrixa Plus enhances feed efficiency, boosts productivity, and ensures sustained performance, making it an essential nutritional solution for modern dairy farming.
