Product Description

  • Shreem Heatrelixa Plus is a specially formulated veterinary solution designed to combat heat stress in poultry birds during high-temperature and humid conditions. Heat stress is one of the major challenges in poultry farming, leading to reduced feed intake, poor growth rate, decreased egg production, and higher mortality. Shreem Heatrelixa Plus helps maintain the bird's physiological balance by supporting thermoregulation, improving hydration status, and enhancing electrolyte balance. It supports better feed conversion ratio (FCR), reduces stress-related weakness, and helps birds maintain normal productivity even under harsh summer conditions.
