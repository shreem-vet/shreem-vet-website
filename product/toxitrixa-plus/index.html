Product Description

  • Shreem Toxitrixa Plus is an advanced liquid toxin binder specially formulated to protect poultry from the harmful effects of mycotoxins and feed contaminants. In modern poultry production, toxin exposure from feed ingredients can lead to poor growth, reduced immunity, liver damage, and decreased productivity. Shreem Toxitrixa Plus works by effectively binding and neutralizing toxins in the gastrointestinal tract, preventing their absorption into the bloodstream. It supports liver function, enhances nutrient utilization, and improves overall bird health and performance. With its fast-acting liquid formulation, Shreem Toxitrixa Plus ensures uniform mixing in drinking water, providing consistent protection across the flock. It is an essential solution for maintaining optimal health, boosting immunity, and maximizing production efficiency in poultry.

Key Benefits

  • Neutralizes Aflatoxins, Ochratoxins, and T-2 toxins.
  • MOS prevents E. coli and Salmonella colonization.
  • Lowers pH to inhibit mold and fungal growth.
  • Choline and B6 protect against Fatty Liver Syndrome.
  • Protein Hydrolysate ensures rapid recovery from toxicoses.
  • Copper Sulphate provides potent protection against fungi.

Indicated in

  • Prevention of mycotoxicosis
  • High moisture or poor quality feed storage
  • Loose droppings, caking of feed or reduced appetite
  • Immunosuppression and frequent disease outbreaks
  • Visible mold growth in feed or raw materials

Dosage

  • In Drinking Water: Mix 1ml/ltr of drinking water (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • In Feed: Use 500ml/ton of feed (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Application Tip: Dilute the required dose with 5lt of water & sprinkle evenly on the feed, mixing thoroughly.
