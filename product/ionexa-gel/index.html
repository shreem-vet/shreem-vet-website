Product Description

  • Shreem Ionexa Gel is an advanced ionic rehydration gel specially formulated for cattle to restore electrolyte balance, boost energy levels, and support rapid recovery during stress, dehydration, heat load, and illness. Enriched with essential electrolytes, energy boosters, and vital nutrients, it ensures quick absorption and immediate action. Ideal for use during transportation, heat stress, post-calving weakness, diarrhea, and reduced feed intake, Shreem Ionexa Gel helps maintain optimum hydration, improves vitality, and enhances overall animal performance. Its easy-to-administer gel format ensures convenience and effective dosing even in weak or off-feed animals.

Key Benefits

  • Strengthens bones

Advantages

  • Prevents Milk Fever, Hypocalcaemia & Rickets.
  • Increase mobilization of calcium from bone to blood.
  • Vitamin D3 ensures maximum absorption of calcium in the intestine.

Dosage

  • Regular USe: 300gm before & 300gm after 6 hours of parturition. (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • To Prevent Milk Fever: Give 300gm after 6 hours of I.V. in fusion, followed by another 300gm after next 6 hours. (or as directed by veterinarian)
