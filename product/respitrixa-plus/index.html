Product Description

  • Shreem Respitrixa Plus is a specialized respiratory support and disinfection solution designed to maintain a healthy respiratory system in poultry. In intensive poultry farming, birds are highly susceptible to respiratory challenges caused by environmental stress, ammonia exposure, dust, and microbial infections, which can severely impact growth and productivity. Shreem Respitrixa Plus works by helping to reduce the microbial load in the respiratory tract while supporting clear airways and improved breathing efficiency. It aids in minimizing respiratory distress, enhancing oxygen utilization, and promoting better overall flock health.

Key Benefits

  • Clears mucus and relieves gasping or rattling sounds.
  • Natural defense against bacterial and viral infections.
  • Reduces body heat and distress during peak summers.
  • Minimizes respiratory stress after vaccinations.
  • Improves oxygen intake for higher energy and growth.

Indicated in

  • Respiratory distress and sneezing.
  • CRD, Coryza, and Bronchitis support.
  • Heat and Environmental stress.
  • Post-vaccination recovery.
  • Improving overall bird performance.

Dosage

  • Chicks: 5/100 birds per day (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Growers: 7ml/per 100 birds per day (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Layers: 10ml/100 birds per day (or as directed by veterinarian)
