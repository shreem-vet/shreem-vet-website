Product Description

  • Shreem Calvion Bolus is a veterinary feed supplement to address calcium deficiencies & boost milk production in livestock. It is a high potency blend of essential minerals, vitamins, jivanti & Shatavari. It strengthens skeletal structure & Supports reproductive health. It prevents milk fever.

Benefits

  • It strengthen bones, teeth & provides structural integrity.
  • It improves the quantity & quality of milk.
  • It helps in the smooth let down of milk.
  • It promotes growth in young animals & maintains high energy level in adults.
  • It improves conception rates & reproductive health.

Indications

  • Low milk yield.
  • Bone weakness.
  • Hypocalcemia.

Dosage

  • Cow/Buffaloes: 1 Bolus twice daily for 7 days. (or as directed by the veterinarian.)
  • Pregnant Animals: 1 Bolus daily for 3-5 days. (preferably before & after calving)
  • Sheep/Goat: 1-2 Bolus daily for 3 days. (or as directed by the veterinarian.)
