Product Description

  • Shreem valin-H is a premium quality Vitamin H (Biotin) liquid supplement specially developed for poultry to support healthy skin, strong feather development, and improved metabolic functions. Biotin plays a crucial role in maintaining carbohydrate, fat, and protein metabolism, thereby enhancing overall growth performance and productivity in birds. Regular supplementation of Shreem valin-H helps in preventing biotin deficiency symptoms such as poor feathering, skin lesions, reduced egg production, leg weakness, and poor feed conversion. It is highly beneficial during rapid growth phases, stress conditions, and recovery periods. Formulated for easy administration through drinking water, Shrevalin-H ensures fast absorption and effective results for a healthier and more productive flock.
