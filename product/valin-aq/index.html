Product Description

  • Shreem valin-AQ is a premium quality Vitamin A liquid formulation specially developed for poultry to support healthy vision, epithelial tissue integrity, and overall immunity. It plays a vital role in improving growth performance, feed efficiency, and resistance against common infections in birds. Formulated with high bioavailable Vitamin A, Shrevalin-AQ helps prevent deficiency-related conditions such as poor eyesight, stunted growth, reduced egg production, and increased susceptibility to respiratory and digestive disorders. It is ideal for routine supplementation as well as during stress conditions, vaccination periods, and recovery phases to ensure optimum flock health and productivity.
