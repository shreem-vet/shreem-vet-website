Product Description

  • Shreem Lactiva Bolus is a nutritional supplement to optimize milk production in diary livestock. It is a blend of essential minerals amino acids & vitamins which addresses the nutritional gaps that cause low milk yield. it boost the quantity of milk, also supports udder health & improves the physiological strength of the animal during lactation cycle.

Benefits

  • Enhances the absorption of calcium & phosphorus for stronger bones & better milk quality.
  • It boosts milk production naturally.
  • Enhances physical strength & animals natural resistance.
  • It reduces the risk of lactation related stress.

Indications

  • Irregularity in milk production
  • Hypogalactia
  • Nutritional deficiency during lactation
  • General weakness in milch animals

Dosage

  • Cow/Buffaloes: 1 Bolus daily for 15 days regular (or as directed by the veterinarian.)
  • Pregnant Animals: 1 Bolus daily for 3 days
  • Goat/Sheep: 1/2 Bolus daily for 3 days
