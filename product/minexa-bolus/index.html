Product Description

  • Shreem Minexa Bolus is a balanced combination of essential macro and micro minerals to bridge the dietary gap in modern livestock farming. It plays a vital role in maintaining growth, reproduction, milk production & immunity in cattle. By providing critical trace elements, it ensure optimal health, prevents minerals deficiency diseases & supports peak performance throughout the lactation & gestation periods.

Benefits

  • Maintain proper metabolic & physiological functions
  • Improve feed utilization & digestion
  • Enhance milk yield & quality
  • Support reproductive efficiency & fertility
  • Promotes faster growth in young stock & improves overall body condition
  • Prevents metabolic disorders like milk fever

Indications

  • Improving milk production
  • Bone development & growth
  • Boosting immunity
  • Prevention of minerals deficiency disorders (milk fever, pica, grass tetany)
