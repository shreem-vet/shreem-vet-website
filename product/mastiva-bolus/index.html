Product Description

  • Shreem Mastiva Bolus is a nutritional approach to maintain udder health during mastitis. It is a combination of vitamins, minerals, probiotics & trisodium citrate to restore milk ph and reduce inflammation. It helps in the regeneration of milk secreting tissues. It is an essential supportive therapy to maintain milk quality & prevent the recurrence of udder related issues.

Benefits

  • It helps in normalize the ph of milk, making it less favorable for bacterial growth.
  • It helps in the rapid repair of udder tissues.
  • It strengthen the natural immunity to fight udder infections.
  • It improves the consistency and colour of milk.
  • It maintains gut health.

Indications

  • udder swelling, pain or hardness
  • Alteration in milk
  • To improve udder health & prevent recurrence of infections
  • Supportive therapy in clinical mastitis

Dosage

  • Large Animal: 1-2 Bolus for 3-5 days daily (or as directed by the veterinarian.)
  • In acute cases, the dose can be repeated or as directed by a veterinary consultant
