Product Description

  • Shrevalin-Cool is a premium quality liquid formulation specially developed for poultry to provide effective relief from heat stress conditions and maintain normal physiological balance during high environmental temperatures. It helps birds stay hydrated, active, and productive even under extreme weather stress. Formulated with a balanced combination of electrolytes, cooling agents, and supportive nutrients, Shrevalin-Cool aids in restoring fluid and mineral balance, reducing body temperature, and improving feed intake and overall performance. It is highly beneficial during summer seasons, transport stress, vaccination stress, and overcrowded housing conditions where birds are prone to dehydration and heat exhaustion.

Key Benefits

  • Effectively lowers core body temperature and minimizes heat-induced panting.
  • Acts as a powerful antioxidant to boost immunity and reduce cortisol levels during stress.
  • Bicarbonate prevents respiratory alkalosis and maintains blood stability.
  • Rapidly replaces vital minerals lost through increased excretion during heat periods.

Dosage

  • General Use: 1gm per 2ltr of water (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Severe Heat Stress: 1gm per 1ltr of water (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Duration: Administer through drinking water for 5-7 days.
