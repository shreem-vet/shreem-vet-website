Product Description

  • Shreem Caltrixa Plus is a scientifically balanced liquid supplement of Calcium and Phosphorus, specially formulated to support strong skeletal development, improved growth, and enhanced productivity in poultry. It plays a vital role in maintaining bone strength, eggshell quality, and overall metabolic functions in birds. In modern poultry production, rapid growth rates and high egg production demand optimal mineral nutrition. Deficiency of calcium and phosphorus can lead to weak bones, poor weight gain, lameness, and reduced eggshell quality. Shreem Caltrixa Plus helps in maintaining the correct mineral balance, ensuring proper bone formation and structural integrity. Its highly bioavailable formulation promotes better absorption and utilization of essential minerals, leading to improved growth performance, stronger bones, and higher production efficiency. Regular supplementation supports healthy, active birds with improved feed conversion and overall farm profitability.

Key Benefits

  • Essential for preventing 'Cage Layer Fatigue' and rickets in fast-growing broilers.
  • High levels of Manganese and Zinc prevent tendon slipping (Perosis) and lameness, ensuring birds stay mobile and reach feed/water easily.
  • Provides breeders with the necessary mineral reserves to ensure strong embryo development and high hatch percentages.
  • Superior eggshell quality, skeletal integrity, leg strength, hatchability booster, and stress recovery.

Dosage

  • Chicks: 15ml/100 birds per day (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Growers: 30ml/100 birds per day (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Layers: 60ml/100 birds per day (or as directed by veterinarian)
