Product Description

  • Shreem Cufrixa Plus is a scientifically formulated respiratory support powder designed to help manage cough, breathing discomfort, and respiratory stress in poultry. It works effectively to soothe irritated airways, reduce congestion, and promote easier breathing, ensuring better comfort and health of birds. Its balanced formulation supports the respiratory system during challenging conditions such as seasonal changes, ammonia exposure, dust, and infectious stress. By improving oxygen intake and reducing respiratory load, Shreem Cufrixa Plus helps maintain optimal growth, feed intake, and overall flock performance.

Key Benefits

  • Quickly thins/expels mucus and relieves gasping and rattling.
  • Dilates air passages for effortless breathing and oxygenation.
  • Herbs provide a protective coating to the respiratory tract.
  • Boosts natural resistance to environmental pathogens.
  • Relieving distress ensures birds continue feeding actively.
