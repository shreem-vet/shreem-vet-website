Product Description

  • Shreem Cuprixa Plus is a scientifically formulated copper sulphate-based solution designed to support optimal hygiene and health management in poultry farms. It plays a vital role in controlling fungal growth, improving water sanitation, and maintaining a cleaner environment for birds. With its strong antimicrobial and antifungal properties, Shreem Cuprixa Plus helps reduce the load of harmful pathogens in drinking water systems, thereby promoting better gut health and overall performance of poultry. It is especially useful during conditions of high humidity and contamination risk, where fungal challenges are common. Manufactured with precision and quality standards by Shreem Vet Industries, this solution ensures reliable efficacy, easy solubility, and safe usage when applied as recommended. It is an essential addition to modern poultry management practices aimed at improving productivity and flock well-being.

Key Benefits

  • It has antibacterial and anti-fungal properties that can help in preventing infections in chickens.
  • Enhances water quality & palatability.
  • It promotes growth in chickens.
  • It helps in absorption of iron which helps in formation of red blood cells in chickens.
  • It boosts immune system of chicken and made them able to fight with infections and diseases.

Indicated in

  • Helps control fungal contamination in drinking water.
  • Supports prevention of crop mycosis and related issues.
  • Improves water hygiene and reduces microbial load.
  • Useful during high humidity and rainy seasons.

Dosage

  • General use: 1-3ml per 2-3 ltr of drinking water (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • Contamination: 3-5ml per 2-3 ltr of drinking water (or as directed by veterinarian)
  • As a Preventive: Use 2-3 times per week (or as directed by veterinarian)
