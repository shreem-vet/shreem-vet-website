Product Description

  • Shreem Bufftrixa Plus is a scientifically balanced rumen buffer formulation designed to maintain optimal rumen pH and prevent acidosis in dairy cattle. High-concentrate feeding and modern dairy practices often lead to rumen imbalance, reduced feed efficiency, and low milk fat—this is where Bufftrixa Plus plays a critical role. Formulated with effective buffering agents and supportive nutrients, it stabilizes rumen conditions, enhances fiber digestion, and promotes better nutrient utilization. This results in improved milk yield, increased milk fat percentage, and overall better animal performance. Regular use of Shreem Bufftrixa Plus helps in reducing digestive disorders, improving feed intake, and ensuring healthier and more productive animals, making it an essential solution for high-performing dairy herds.

Key Benefits

  • Maintains optimal rumen pH balance and effectively prevents acidosis.
  • Increases milk yield and milk fat percentage
  • Improves feed efficiency and nutrient absorption

Advantages

  • Enhances fiber digestion for better utilization of roughage.

  • Supports healthy rumen microflora activity

  • Helps in stabilizing rumen conditions during high concentrate feeding
  • Improves overall animal performance and productivity
  • Suitable for lactating, high-yielding, and stress-prone cattle

Dosage
