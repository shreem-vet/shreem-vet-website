Product Description

  • Shreem Grovtrixa Plus is a scientifically formulated growth promoter designed to enhance growth rate, feed efficiency, and overall productivity in dairy cattle. It combines essential nutrients, growth-supporting factors, and metabolic enhancers to ensure optimal development and performance. This advanced formulation helps improve nutrient utilization, supports healthy digestion, and promotes faster body weight gain in growing animals. In lactating cattle, it contributes to better milk production and sustained performance by improving overall metabolic efficiency. Regular use of Shreem Grovtrixa Plus leads to stronger, healthier, and more productive animals, making it an ideal solution for modern livestock farming focused on profitability and performance.
