Product Description

  • Shreem Toxitrixa Plus is a scientifically developed toxin binder designed to effectively adsorb and neutralize harmful mycotoxins present in poultry feed. Its advanced formulation helps protect vital organs such as liver and intestines from toxin damage, ensuring better health and productivity of birds. By binding a wide range of mycotoxins and preventing their absorption in the gastrointestinal tract, Shreem Toxitrixa Plus supports improved nutrient utilization, stronger immunity, and enhanced feed efficiency. It plays a crucial role in reducing the negative impact of contaminated feed, including poor growth, reduced egg production, and immunosuppression.

Key Benefits

  • HSCAS and Charcoal trap Aflatoxins and chemical toxins.
  • Calcium Propionate prevents mold and fungal growth in feed.
  • MOS blocks harmful bacteria like E. coli from the gut wall.
  • Copper Sulphate provides intensive protection against fungi.

Indicated in

  • Prevention of Mycotoxicosis (Aflatoxin, Ochratoxin, etc.).
  • High moisture or poor quality feed storage.
  • Loose droppings, caking of feed, or reduced appetite.
  • Immunosuppression and frequent disease outbreaks.
  • Visible mold growth in feed or raw materials.

Dosage

  • Mix 500gm-1kg per MT of feed or as directed by veterinarian
