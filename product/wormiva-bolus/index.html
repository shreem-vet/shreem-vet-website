Product Description

  • Shreem Wormiva Bolus is a specially formulated veterinary products design to control & eliminate internal parasites in cattle. This bolus works by targeting and removing harmful worms from the gastro intestinal tract & other organs, thereby helping to restore normal digestion & improve nutrient absorption. It plays a vital role in maintaining optimum body condition, better immunity, higher milk yield & a shiny coat in diary cattle.

Benefits

  • Helps in natural control of internal parasites.
  • Improves digestion & gut health.
  • Enhances feed absorption & appetite.
  • Supports overall immunity & productivity.
  • Safe for regular use as supplement.

Indications

  • Weight loss or poor growth.
  • Rough hair coat.
  • Diarrhea or digestive disturbances.
  • Reduced milk yield.
  • Visible signs of worm infestation.

Dosage

  • Large Animals: 1-2 Bolus as a single dose. Give 2 bolus in the evening 1 hour after feeding fodder, do not give anything else afterwards.
  • Small Animals: 1 Bolus as a single dose. Give 1 bolus in the evening 1 hour after feeding fodder, do not give anything else afterwards.
  • Heifer/Calves: 1 Bolus as a single dose. Repeat the dose every three months or as directed by a veterinary consultant.
